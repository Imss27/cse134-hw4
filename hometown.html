My favourite place, Chengdu

Chengdu is famous for two things: PANDAS and HOTPOT

Chengdu, the capital of Sichuan province in southwestern China, is a city known for its rich history, delicious cuisine, and the harmonious coexistence of tradition and modernity. Renowned for its spicy Sichuan dishes, the city offers a unique culinary experience. It's home to ancient temples, modern skyscrapers, and a laid-back atmosphere, making it a captivating destination for travelers seeking an authentic taste of Chinese culture and urban experiences. Chengdu also serves as the gateway to the Giant Panda Sanctuary in Sichuan, adding to its allure as a must-visit city in China.
